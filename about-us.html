About Us

Stone Security Services was founded by David Stone who left the NYPD to pursue his vision of developing a premium security organization that would meet the growing demand for high-level protective services in New York City. Stone Security Services is among the first security firms to effectively utilize the services of off-duty police officers.

Stone Security Services is a security provider that secures over 900 events per year. Besides events, we secure the celebrity, royal family, and corporate client as well. Our management experience can handle any event from 100 to 3,000 guests. Our pinnacle customer service makes us one of the most called upon security services in New York, Los Angeles, & Miami. Stone Security has developed to be the preferred choice for Event Planners, Production Companies, Public Relations Firms, and Venues. We secure every type of event ranging from Red Carpet Premieres, Product Launches, Charity Galas, Fashion Shows.


Stone Security Services is dedicated to becoming the premier leader for the demanding corporate and private client needs. Stone Security Service in New York forges a special relationship with each of its clients to assure the highest degree of attention to the protection of client assets and to provide the most cost effective deployment of its capabilities and services. We are deeply committed to solutions that optimize the best human resources to always meet or exceed our clients’ highest goals and expectations. “Our business is minding your business.”
