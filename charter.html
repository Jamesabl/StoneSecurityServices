Charter with Captain Clay

Join Captain Clay for the fishing experience of a lifetime! Gather your party of 4-5 and come with the captain to the best fishing locations here near Delray Beach! He'll take you on his boat for four hours, where he supplies the bait, gear, and guidance to make sure you and your party have a memory for the books. Book your charter today by calling Captain Clay at (561) 512-0274.





About the Captain

Captain Clay S. Brand is a Florida native who lived on the island of Dominica for many years before returning to South Florida where he graduated with a bachelors degree in business administration from Florida Atlantic University and is a US Coast Guard licensed captain.
Captain Clay has been a commercial fisherman in the area for over forty years and would love for you to join him on his next charter!
