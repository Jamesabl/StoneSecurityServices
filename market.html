Welcome to Our Markets!


Come to our markets, here in Downtown Delray Beach, Florida and check out our many seafood options! We have a huge assortment of all of the most fresh and delicious fish, caught by trusted and experienced fisherman throughout out the local Palm Beach area. Ranging from the delcious and exclusive Pumpkin Swordfish to Yellowtail Snapper to Mahi-Mahi, we're sure to have the fresh seafood you're looking for!
