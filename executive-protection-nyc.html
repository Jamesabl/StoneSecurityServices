NYC Executive Protection

Stone Security Service in New York offers a full range of discreet, personal, and professional Executive Protection Services. Our security services, from event, diplomatic, and estate security to bodyguard services, are all designed to minimize risk and intrusion while maximizing personal protection without limiting mobility.

Today’s global executives, celebrities, and high-profile individuals face unprecedented corporate and personal risks requiring an advanced level of security coordination, planning, and management. The sophistication and determination of potential adversaries who would exploit such exposures of these persons, their organizations, or their families are unfortunately cunning. Stone Security Service NYC Executive Protection ensures safety and discretion, respecting your need for privacy and security. In this world, providing unobtrusive but effective levels of protection at work, home, or travel is paramount.


The need for executives and high-net-worth individuals to move swiftly and securely throughout today’s business arena often overburdens the capabilities and resources of most corporate security departments. Stone Security implements customized executive protection planning to establish the optimal level of security for the individual at home, at work, and while on travel or in public.
